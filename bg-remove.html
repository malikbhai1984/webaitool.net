üí° Professional Tips for Best Results

  • Use Good Lighting: Images with clear subject separation and good lighting produce the best results. Avoid heavily shadowed or dark images.
  • High Resolution Works Best: Higher resolution images (1000px+) allow the AI to detect edges more precisely, especially for hair and fur.
  • Center Your Subject: Position the main subject in the center of the frame for optimal AI detection and processing.
  • Avoid Complex Backgrounds: While our AI handles complex backgrounds well, simpler backgrounds (solid colors, minimal patterns) process fastest.
  • Check File Size: Keep images under 10MB. If larger, resize or compress before uploading for faster processing.
  • Save as PNG: Always download as PNG to preserve transparency. Don't convert to JPG as it doesn't support transparent backgrounds.
  • Portraits Work Great: Our AI excels at removing backgrounds from portraits, detecting even fine hair details accurately.
